About Me


I used to be blown away by reversing a string in Python for detecting a palindrome, now I've seen data science be used to detect earthquakes and breast cancer, as well as optimize business processes and logistics through the current AI revolution. Every day I grow more impressed with the limitless capacity for data scientists and what they can accomplish, and being apart of that is something that I constantly strive for.

I am a third year Business Analytics & Computer Science student at the University of Texas at Austin, and I'm always learning more about the intricacies of data science as I attempt to navigate the massive field of data and become an expert in it myself.
